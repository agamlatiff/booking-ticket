help Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my flight date?

expand_more
To change your flight date, go to "My Bookings", select the flight you wish to change, and click "Modify Flight". Please note that change fees may apply depending on your ticket type.

What is the baggage allowance for economy class?

expand_more
Economy class passengers are typically allowed one carry-on bag (up to 7kg) and one checked bag (up to 23kg). However, this can vary by airline and route. Please check your ticket details for specific information.

How can I request a refund?

expand_more
Refunds can be requested through the "Cancellations & Refunds" section in your account. If your flight was cancelled by the airline, you are entitled to a full refund. Processing times usually take 7-14 business days.

Do I need a visa for my connecting flight?

expand_more
Visa requirements for connecting flights depend on the country you are transiting through and your citizenship. We recommend checking with the embassy or using our "Travel Requirements" tool before you fly.
