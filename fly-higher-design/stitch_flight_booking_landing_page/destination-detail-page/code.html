About Paris

Paris, France's capital, is a major European city and a global center for art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. Its 19th-century cityscape is crisscrossed by wide boulevards and the River Seine. Beyond such towering landmarks as the Eiffel Tower and the 12th-century, Gothic Notre-Dame cathedral, the city is known for its cafe culture and designer boutiques along the Rue du Faubourg Saint-Honoré.

Whether you’re marveling at the Mona Lisa in the Louvre, strolling through the Tuileries Garden, or enjoying a croissant in a street-side café in Montmartre, Paris offers an unforgettable experience steeped in history and romance.
